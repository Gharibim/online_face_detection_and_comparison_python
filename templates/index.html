About

Face detection and comparission. In this project, you will be able to display a streaming video from your webcam, take a screenshot using a 'screenshot' button then compare the captured face to the criminals faces (in this project, the criminals faces are only Mohamed's face. Nothing else in the database). Then the result will let you know if the captured face is a criminal or not

This project is about capturing criminals faces through a streaming video camera then compare these faces to the criminals faces in the database. the results will be whether the captured face is a criminal or not!
